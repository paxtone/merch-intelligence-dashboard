🔄 Repeat Flywheel: 44.5% Repeat Rate · 504 Repeat Orders
504 repeat orders drove 44.5% of WK09 volume. The explosive 2nd-order cohort (199 orders, up from 17 in 2025) signals the Nomad Sling launch is pulling new buyers back. Revenue hit $105.7K — highest weekly total of 2026.
💰 Paid Media: Total Paid $29.2K · Meta ROAS Dipped on Spend Ramp
Meta spend nearly doubled to $14.6K (from $7.4K WK08), pushing ROAS down to 4.61x from 5.98x. All Bags ($2,598, 5.0x ROAS) and Advantage+ ($1,394, 6.37x) are top performers. Google at $14.6K with $42.40 CPA.
🔍 SEO: Impressions -28% Over 7 Weeks · CTR Recovering
Organic impressions declined to 125K (from 174K in WK02) but CTR recovered to 1.41%. "Star wars shirts" leads at 18 clicks. "Spiderman shirt" surging to pos 6.6. "Star wars merch" slipped to pos 17.1.
📈 Star Wars = 74.8% Revenue · $79K in One Week
Star Wars: $79K of $106K (74.8%), 1,309 of 1,995 units (65.6%). Critical Role had a strong week at $7.0K (289 units). DC Batman Nomad Sling sold 32 units. Mandalorian Nomad Sling continues dominating at 103 units.
